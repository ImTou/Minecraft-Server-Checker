Donate Us

Thank you for visiting the donation page for Minecraft Server Checker, my GitHub project aimed at helping Minecraft players quickly and easily check the status of their favorite servers. Developing and maintaining this tool takes a lot of time and effort, and any support you can offer is greatly appreciated. By making a donation through Ko-fi, you are not only helping to ensure that this project remains free and accessible for all, but also showing your appreciation for the hard work that goes into creating useful tools for the Minecraft community. Every contribution, no matter how small, goes a long way in helping me continue to improve and expand Minecraft Server Checker. Thank you for your support!
